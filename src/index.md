I'm an Associate Architect for the SAP Cloud BTP platform, specializing in high-performance PostgreSQL and Redis services. Based in Bangalore, India, my work bridges cloud-native infrastructure with scalable enterprise solutions.

With over 10 years of experience, my engineering background spans Cloud-Native IoT SaaS applications, AI platforms, and legacy modernization across the Automotive and Healthcare domains. I hold 2 patents on IoT and digital twins.

Previously, I was a Senior Technical Lead at Continental AG architecting scalable IoT SaaS platforms. Before that, I spent 6 years at IBM Software Labs focusing on AI Applications and IoT Cloud tooling, and developed Teleradiology PACS Web applications at Medsynaptic.

Currently, outside of my SAP work, my focus is on contributing to open-source projects and exploring different AI and tech stack approaches.

My core technical stack includes Node.js, Java, Python, Go, and Rust. I believe in brutalist, text-heavy architectures that prioritize performance and developer experience over unnecessary abstraction.
